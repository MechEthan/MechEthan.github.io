Autonomous Indy Car Steering Control

One of the most challenging projects of my undergraduate degree. Created a dynamic model of inputs and outputs then developed a controller to steer the Indy Car around Indianapolis Motor Speedway with a settle time under 0.6 seconds and a max percent overshoot of 5%. Our final reselts were imperfect but was partial caused by an error in the project set-up.
