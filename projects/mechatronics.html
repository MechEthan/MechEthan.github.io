Mechatronics Project

One of my favorite courses while at Auburn was mechatronics. This project was the culmination of the course combining everything learned during the semester. It required coding a stepper motor, LCD display, and Keypad (with encoder) with PIC basic to complete a sequense determined by the professor. I strongly believe the best way to learn to build hardware and code is through projects. I can make mistakes and sometimes it feels like one step forward, two steps back but at least I am always making some progress and learning.
