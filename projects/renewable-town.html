Renewable Energy Town

Developed a renewable energy system to cover all the cooling, heating, water heating, and electric power of "Ren-Town." My "Ren-Town" was located in Honolulu, Hawaii.

Based on a sample home's energy use we developed an OTEC System, which uses water temperature to produce energy, and wind turbines to fully power Ren-Town. Additionally we determined costs, payback periods, and envirnmental effects.

Goals

  • Determine Energy requirnments for the location
  • Design viable energy solutions
  • Optimize power output and payback period

Tools & Skills Used

E.g., CAD, MATLAB, Arduino, etc.
